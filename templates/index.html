How It Works

1

Report Lost Item

Report your lost item through the system with detailed description including color, brand, location where it was lost, and unique features. You can also upload a photo for better identification.

2

Found Item Submission

If you find an item on campus, report it through the system and submit it to the admin office. The admin office acts as a centralized collection point for all found items.

3

AI Matching & Verification

Our AI system automatically matches lost and found items. When a match is found, administrators verify the details, confirm identity of the owner, and facilitate the return process.

4

Item Return & Update

Once verification is complete, the admin office returns the item to you and updates the system status. You receive notifications about your item's status throughout the process.

System Features

Advanced Search: Search for lost or found items using keywords, categories, locations, and dates. Filter results by type, status, and time period for precise matching.
Photo Upload: Attach images to your reports for visual identification. Photos help administrators and the system better identify and match your items.
Location Tracking: Record exact locations where items were lost or found. This information helps administrators organize items and improve matching accuracy.
Automatic Timestamps: All reports are automatically timestamped when created. Track when items were lost or found with exact date and time records.
Admin Office Hub: All found items are collected at the centralized admin office. Administrators verify ownership, manage inventory, and coordinate returns safely.
Secure Verification: Administrators verify identity details before returning items. The system maintains detailed records of ownership verification and item transfers.
Email Notifications: Receive instant email updates when matches are found, your item status changes, or when you need to collect your item from the admin office.
Status Updates: Track your item's journey from lost/found to verification, collection, and return. Real-time status updates keep you informed at every stage.
